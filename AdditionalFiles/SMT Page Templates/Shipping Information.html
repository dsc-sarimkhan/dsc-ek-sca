Shipping Policy

 

At EvaKool we strive to provide friendly and comprehensive support to our valued customers. Here are some answers to more commonly asked questions that we receive.

 

For further assistance, please contact 1300 385 665 or email sales@evakool.com for shipping and product information or warranty@evakool.com for technical assistance.

 

SHIPPING

 

Orders placed on the website normally have a 1-2 business day handling time and shipping will vary depending on location. Product normally ships from our Caloundra, QLD factory. You can also opt to collect the item from Caloundra to save on shipping. Please note we will only hold items for 7 days for your collection. You can view our warehouse opening hours for collection on our website.

 

NEW PRODUCTS

 

We will at all times endeavour to ensure that our stock listings on our website are up to date. However at times there may be unforeseeable shortages and therefore all items listed on the website are Subject to Availability.

 

Our standard procedure will be to call you if there are any shortages in your order and advise you when those items will be available.

 

RED DOT / RECONDITIONED PRODUCTS

 

We do not manufacture seconds and they are a randomly occurring product, quantities of which may vary on a day to day basis. We will monitor stock quantities and will update the status on the website daily, however in view of the nature of seconds all items on our seconds website are Subject to Availability.

 

Our standard procedure will be to call you if there are any shortages in your order and advise you when those items will be available.

 

PO BOXES

 

We cannot ship to PO boxes, all of our website products are shipped via courier and can only be sent to a physical street address. We advise selecting a delivery address that is regularly attended during business hours. Any undelivered items will be returned to your local StarTrack depot for collection.

 

OTHER

 

If FREE SHIPPING or DISCOUNTED SHIPPING is advertised in a promotion or on a product it is valid within Australia only and only on the products as described in the promotion when purchased through the Evakool website. Some remote and/or offshore postcodes may attract a shipping charge at Evakool's discretion. If this is the case we will notify you either by email or phone. If you believe you may be in one of these areas and wish to check please contact Evakool.

 

Shipping to remote locations may incur a higher fee. All pricing for freight is subject to change at Evakool's discretion. Evakool will call you if there are any issues with the freight you have been charged.
